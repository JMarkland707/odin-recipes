Odin's Thicc Chocolate Chip Cookies

These are the great, classic chocolate chip cookies you know and love, packed with gooey goodness. The hot fudge center stays soft even after your cookies have fully cooled.

Odin strikes again. These Chocolate Chip Cookies are easy to make and easier to eat. The thick chocolate chunks and gooey center will leave you craving more.

    Ingredients
  • ½ cup hot fudge topping
  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour, plus more for dusting
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • ¾ cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 ounce) package semisweet chocolate chips
