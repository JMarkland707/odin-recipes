Odin's Honey-Garlic Chicken Thighs

Easy set it and forget it recipe. Odin's Honey-Garlic Slow Cooker Chicken Thighs

Once again, Odin proves why he is the culinary mastermind of all the Norse Gods.

    Ingredients
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken thighs
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ½ cup ketchup
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    Directions
  1. Lay chicken thighs into the bottom of a 4-quart slow cooker.
  2. Whisk soy sauce, ketchup, honey, garlic, and basil together in a bowl; pour over the chicken.
  3. Cook on Low for 6 hours
